# Hotel AI Receptionist - Comprehensive Feature Plan

## Overview
A 24/7 AI-powered voice receptionist designed to handle all guest interactions via phone, providing instant responses to inquiries, managing bookings, and coordinating hotel services with human-like conversation quality.

---

## 1. HOTEL INFORMATION & INQUIRIES

### 1.1 General Hotel Information
**Description**: Provide comprehensive information about the hotel's identity, location, and basic details.

**Capabilities**:
- Hotel name, address, and contact information
- Operating hours (if applicable, e.g., lobby desk hours)
- Hotel star rating and classification
- Brief history and unique selling points
- Awards and certifications
- Ownership and management information
- Sister properties and chain affiliations

**Conversation Flow**:
- Natural language query handling: "Tell me about your hotel"
- Specific detail requests: "What's your address?"
- Comparison questions: "Are you part of a chain?"

---

### 1.2 Check-In & Check-Out Policies
**Description**: Handle all timing and policy-related questions for guest arrivals and departures.

**Capabilities**:
- Standard check-in time (e.g., 3:00 PM)
- Standard check-out time (e.g., 11:00 AM)
- Early check-in availability and fees
- Late check-out options and charges
- Express check-in/check-out procedures
- Online check-in process explanation
- Required documents (ID, credit card, etc.)
- deposit and payment policies
- Age requirements for check-in
- Group check-in procedures

**Conversation Flow**:
- Direct queries: "What time is check-in?"
- Conditional requests: "Can I check in early at 10 AM?"
- Policy clarifications: "Do you need a deposit?"

---

### 1.3 Room Information
**Description**: Detailed information about all room types, configurations, and amenities.

**Capabilities**:
- **Room Categories**:
  - Standard rooms
  - Deluxe rooms
  - Suites (Junior, Executive, Presidential)
  - Family rooms
  - Accessible rooms
  - Connecting rooms

- **Room Features**:
  - Bed configurations (King, Queen, Twin, Sofa bed)
  - Room size (square footage/meters)
  - Maximum occupancy
  - View types (Ocean, City, Garden, Pool)
  - Floor levels available

- **In-Room Amenities**:
  - Wi-Fi (speed and coverage)
  - TV (size, channels, smart features)
  - Air conditioning/heating controls
  - Mini-bar contents and pricing
  - Coffee/tea making facilities
  - Safe box specifications
  - Bathroom amenities (toiletries, hairdryer, bathrobe)
  - Workspace setup
  - Balcony/terrace availability

**Conversation Flow**:
- Comparison questions: "What's the difference between deluxe and standard rooms?"
- Specific needs: "Do you have rooms with two beds?"
- Accessibility: "Tell me about wheelchair-accessible rooms"

## 3. BOOKING MANAGEMENT

### 3.1 New Booking Creation
**Description**: Complete booking process from inquiry to confirmation.

**Capabilities**:
- **Availability Checking**:
  - Real-time room availability search
  - Date range flexibility ("I need a room around December 15th")
  - Room type availability
  - Rate comparison across dates

- **Reservation Details Collection**:
  - Guest name (spelling confirmation)
  - Contact information (phone, email)
  - Arrival and departure dates
  - Room type preference
  - Number of guests (adults/children)
  - Special requests (high floor, quiet room, etc.)
  - Bed preference
  - Smoking/non-smoking

- **Rate Information**:
  - Best available rate presentation
  - Package deals and promotions
  - Seasonal pricing explanation
  - Discount codes application
  - Corporate or group rates
  - Loyalty program benefits

- **Payment Processing**:
  - Credit card information collection (secure)
  - Payment authorization
  - Deposit requirements
  - Prepayment vs. pay-at-hotel options
  - Currency acceptance
  - Cancellation policy explanation

- **Confirmation**:
  - Booking confirmation number generation
  - Email confirmation sending
  - SMS confirmation option
  - Reservation summary recitation
  - Modifications and cancellation policy reiteration

**Conversation Flow**:
- Natural booking: "I'd like to book a room for next weekend"
- Guided process: System asks for each detail sequentially
- Upselling: "Would you like to upgrade to an ocean view?"

---

### 3.2 Booking Modifications
**Description**: Handle changes to existing reservations.

**Capabilities**:
- **Retrieval**:
  - Find booking by confirmation number
  - Find booking by guest name and dates
  - Find booking by phone number or email

- **Modification Types**:
  - Date changes (subject to availability)
  - Room type changes
  - Guest count changes
  - Add/remove special requests
  - Update contact information
  - Add extras (breakfast, parking, etc.)

- **Fee Handling**:
  - Calculate change fees if applicable
  - Explain rate differences
  - Process additional payments
  - Issue refunds if needed

- **Reconfirmation**:
  - Send updated confirmation
  - Verify new details with guest
  - Update all systems

**Conversation Flow**:
- Change request: "I need to move my reservation to next week"
- Policy explanation: "There's no fee, but the rate is $20 higher"
- Confirmation: "I've updated your reservation, you'll receive a new confirmation"

---

### 3.3 Booking Inquiries
**Description**: Provide information about existing reservations.

**Capabilities**:
- **Status Checking**:
  - Confirm booking exists
  - Verify dates and room type
  - Check payment status
  - Review special requests on file

- **Information Provision**:
  - Rate paid
  - Cancellation deadline
  - Included amenities
  - Upgrade availability
  - Current offers for extending stay

- **Pre-Arrival Information**:
  - Directions to hotel
  - Parking information
  - Check-in procedure
  - What to bring
  - Local weather forecast
  - Nearby attractions

**Conversation Flow**:
- Verification: "Can you confirm my reservation for this Friday?"
- Details: "What room type did I book?"
- Preparation: "What time should I arrive?"

---

### 3.4 Cancellations
**Description**: Process booking cancellations according to policy.

**Capabilities**:
- **Policy Application**:
  - Check cancellation deadline
  - Calculate penalties if applicable
  - Explain refund process
  - Offer alternatives (rescheduling, credit)

- **Processing**:
  - Cancel reservation in system
  - Process refund to original payment method
  - Send cancellation confirmation
  - Update availability

- **Recovery Attempts**:
  - Ask reason for cancellation
  - Offer solutions if applicable (room change, rate adjustment)
  - Suggest future stays
  - Maintain positive relationship

**Conversation Flow**:
- Request: "I need to cancel my reservation"
- Policy: "You're within the free cancellation period, I can cancel with no penalty"
- Completion: "Your reservation is cancelled, and you'll receive a full refund in 5-7 business days"

## 4. SERVICE REQUESTS

### 4.1 Room Service Orders
**Description**: Take and process food and beverage orders for in-room delivery.

**Capabilities**:
- **Menu Navigation**:
  - Present available menu items
  - Describe dishes and ingredients
  - Suggest popular items
  - Accommodate dietary restrictions
  - Explain portion sizes

- **Order Taking**:
  - Collect item selections
  - Note modifications (no onions, extra sauce, etc.)
  - Confirm quantities
  - Suggest pairings and add-ons
  - Offer beverages

- **Delivery Details**:
  - Collect room number
  - Confirm guest name
  - Set delivery time expectations
  - Offer ASAP or scheduled delivery
  - Provide order tracking if available

- **Payment**:
  - Charge to room
  - Explain service charges and gratuity
  - Provide total cost
  - Confirm billing

**Conversation Flow**:
- Order: "I'd like to order room service"
- Details: "I'll have the Caesar salad with grilled chicken, no croutons"
- Timing: "It will be delivered to room 305 in approximately 30 minutes"

---

### 4.2 Housekeeping Requests
**Description**: Handle requests for cleaning and room maintenance.

**Capabilities**:
- **Service Types**:
  - Additional towels/linens
  - Extra pillows or blankets
  - Toiletry replenishment
  - Room cleaning (immediate or scheduled)
  - Turndown service
  - Bed making
  - Trash removal

- **Scheduling**:
  - Immediate requests
  - Scheduled cleaning times
  - Do not disturb respect
  - Make-up room while guest is out

- **Request Logging**:
  - Room number collection
  - Specific item quantities
  - Urgency level
  - Special instructions
  - Estimated completion time

**Conversation Flow**:
- Request: "Can I get more towels?"
- Details: "How many would you like?"
- Confirmation: "I've sent the request, housekeeping will deliver 4 towels to room 402 within 15 minutes"

---

### 4.3 Maintenance Requests
**Description**: Report and track room issues and repairs.

**Capabilities**:
- **Issue Reporting**:
  - AC/heating problems
  - Plumbing issues
  - Electrical problems
  - TV or internet connectivity
  - Lock or key issues
  - Broken fixtures or furniture
  - Noise complaints

- **Urgency Assessment**:
  - Classify severity (emergency vs. routine)
  - Immediate safety concerns
  - Comfort impact
  - Prioritization

- **Resolution**:
  - Dispatch maintenance staff
  - Provide estimated response time
  - Offer room change if severe
  - Follow up confirmation
  - Guest satisfaction check

**Conversation Flow**:
- Report: "The air conditioning isn't working in my room"
- Response: "I'm dispatching maintenance to room 508 right away"
- Follow-up: "Is there anything else I can help you with while you wait?"
